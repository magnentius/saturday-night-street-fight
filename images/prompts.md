# Saturday Night Street Fight — Asset Generation Prompts

This file stores recommended text prompts for generating visual assets (cover art, character portraits, environment blocks) for the game, all matching the gritty **1970s NYC urban decay** aesthetic.

---

## 1. Album Cover Art (Vintage Vinyl Sleeve)

Used to generate the official cover art for the game rules.

```text
A vintage 1970s vinyl record album cover jacket sleeve, square format. The cardboard cover shows realistic ringwear distress, scuffed edges, and retro aging. The artwork is a gritty hand-painted 1970s cinematic illustration of a rain-slicked New York City street corner at night. Steam rises from a manhole cover. Two street fighters, a boxer in a leather jacket and a martial artist, clash in a dynamic fight stance. The background features graffiti-covered brick walls and a flickering neon sign. Gritty film grain, vintage color palette with warm oranges, reds, and cool shadows, chiaroscuro lighting, retro 70s aesthetic. Absolutely NO text, NO words, NO titles, and NO letters anywhere on the cover.
```

## 2. Environment Exploration Blocks

Prompts for generating environmental blocks and landmark backdrops for campaign sessions.

### A. The Subway Platform (Danger Zone Stop)
```text
A gritty, low-angle 1970s film photo of an abandoned New York City subway station platform at night. The walls are covered in colorful layers of spray-painted graffiti. Dim, flickering fluorescent lights overhead cast long shadows. Water drips from the ceiling into small puddles on the concrete floor. Empty turnstiles and iron exit gates stand near the exits. High-contrast chiaroscuro lighting, heavy film grain, cinematic 70s aesthetics.
```

### B. The Greasy Spoon Diner (Safe Haven)
```text
A hand-painted 1970s movie still of a small, warm greasy spoon diner in NYC at 2 AM. Outside the window, a wet, dark street is visible. Inside, a long vinyl counter with chrome stools, warm light glowing from pendant lamps, and steam rising from coffee pots behind the counter. High-contrast shadows, rich warm orange and brown color palette, textured film grain, retro vintage look.
```

### C. The Alleyway Brawl Spot
```text
A high-contrast, hand-painted 1970s grindhouse film poster landscape. A narrow, dark New York City alleyway wedged between two brick tenement buildings. Rusted iron fire escapes cling to the walls. Oversized green metal dumpsters and wooden crates block the path. Puddles reflect the dim amber glow of a single hanging lightbulb. Steam rises from sewer vents, heavy film grain, dark shadows, moody vintage atmosphere.
```
